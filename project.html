Kashmir to Kanyakumari

It is a tourism website where you can choose different kinds of tours, such as adventures, pilgrimages, or heritage-based. It also suggests you places which are points of tourist attraction, where you can experience adrenaline rushes or amazing heritage sites in India, and explore the past through scriptures and monuments. You can also visit various religious sites and can be redirected to some services that provide tours to those locations.

Encrypt it

This webpage is an interactive encryption and decryption tool. It enables users to input a message, a key (ranging from 0 to 52), and then decide whether to encrypt or decrypt the message. The encryption and decryption process relies on the user-supplied key. The encrypted or decrypted message is then displayed in separate fields for the user to view the results.
